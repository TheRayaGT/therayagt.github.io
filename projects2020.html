Pythagoras and Ptolemy (Jul 2020 - Aug 2020)

As part of a series of sharing sessions on Geometry by different members of the college's Mathematics Club, my presentation is on two fundamental theorems, namely Ptolemy's Theorem and Pythagoras' Theorem. This allowed the audience to have a brief taster on geometrical proofs and some trivia about the two theorems mentioned.
