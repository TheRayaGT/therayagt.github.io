About Me

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Mathematics at the National University of Singapore. My current interests in Mathematics are Linear Algebra, Number Theory as well as Real and Complex Analysis. My love for problem-solving has driven my accomplishments in various Mathematics competitions over the years, both locally and internationally. Also in the field of Computer Science, I'm mainly interested in Artificial Intelligence and Computer Graphics.

Beyond my academic pursuits in Mathematics and Computer Science, I am also captivated by certain aspects of Geography, particularly in the areas of Tourism, Coastal Management, and Sustainable Development. I believe that understanding the dynamics of our planet is essential for devising sustainable solutions and preserving our environment for future generations.

If you share similar interests or see potential collaboration opportunities, I would be delighted to connect with you. Let's engage in meaningful conversations and explore how we can make a difference together.

Photography

Here is a compilation of shots that I captured over the years. I love architectural photography and landscape photography as they offer numerous opportunities to seamlessly blend geometry into my pictures. I have had experience working with different cameras, including the GoPro HERO5, Canon 750D, Canon 80D, Canon 5D Mark IV and Sony a7 III.

In mid-2017, I began my journey in photography and videography. Unfortunately, I failed to back up most of my old files during that time. As a result, my collection only dates back to 2019.
